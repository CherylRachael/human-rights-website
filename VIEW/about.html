About Us

Background Information

The Universal Declaration of Human Rights (UDHR) is a milestone document in the history of human rights. Drafted by representatives with different legal and cultural backgrounds from all regions of the world, it sets out fundamental human rights to be universally protected.

Human rights education is essential for fostering a culture of respect and equality. It helps individuals understand their rights and responsibilities, promoting a just and inclusive society.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to promote awareness and understanding of human rights through education, advocacy, and community engagement. We aim to empower individuals and communities to recognize and assert their rights, contributing to a more just and equitable world.

The hackathon aims to bring together creative minds to develop innovative solutions for human rights challenges. Our goals include raising awareness, fostering collaboration, and driving impactful change.
